User Attributes for ABAC

These attributes are included in the JWT token and sent to OPA for policy evaluation:

Role

Defines the user's permission level: admin, manager, or employee.

Department

Used for department-based access control: engineering, hr, finance, etc.

Designation

Job title that can be used for fine-grained access decisions.
